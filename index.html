About Me

Hi, I'm Anna, a photographer, mom, and nature lover based in Snoqualmie, Washington.

Photography has been my passion for over 15 years — a creative journey that has shaped how I see people, light, and connection. As a mother, I'm especially drawn to genuine emotions and meaningful moments, the kind that can't be staged but deserve to be remembered.

I love meeting new people and creating a relaxed, welcoming experience where you can simply be yourself. I believe photographs grow more valuable with time, turning fleeting moments into lasting memories you'll return to for years. While Snoqualmie is home, I'm happy to travel to capture your story wherever it unfolds — so you don't just see your photos, you truly feel them.
