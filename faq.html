Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your session

How do I book a session?

A non-refundable retainer ($50) is required to reserve your date and time. The remaining balance is due on or before the session date.

What is included in the session price?

Each session includes the photography time listed, professional editing in my signature style, and delivery of the specified number of high-resolution edited images.

What should we wear for our session?

I recommend coordinating outfits in soft, neutral tones that complement each other without being too matchy. Avoid busy patterns and logos. I'm happy to provide personalized wardrobe guidance before your session!

Do you offer rush delivery?

Yes. Rush delivery is available upon request for an additional fee and must be arranged prior to your session.

Can I purchase additional images or prints?

Absolutely. Additional images and professional, high-quality prints are available for purchase for all session types.

Do you travel for sessions?

Yes, absolutely! While I'm based in Snoqualmie, WA, I love traveling for sessions. Travel within my local service area (Bellevue to North Bend) is included. Sessions outside this range may require a small travel fee based on distance. Please contact me for a custom quote.

What if I need to reschedule or the weather is bad?

Outdoor sessions are weather-dependent. If weather conditions are unfavorable, we'll reschedule at no additional charge. Rescheduling requests should be made as early as possible.

Are RAW or unedited images included?

No. RAW or unedited images are not provided. All delivered images are fully edited and curated to reflect my artistic style and quality standards.

What usage rights do I receive?

Portrait, senior, elopement, and family sessions include personal usage rights. Commercial usage (including business branding, events, or marketing) may require additional licensing.

Is sales tax included in the price?

Sales tax may apply where required by law and will be added to the final invoice if applicable.
