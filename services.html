Photography Services Agreement

By booking a photography session, the Client agrees to the following terms and conditions:

1. Booking & Retainer: A non-refundable retainer ($50) is required to secure the session date and time. The remaining balance is due on or before the session date. Sessions are not considered booked until the retainer is received.

2. Services & Deliverables: The session includes photography time as outlined in the selected package and delivery of the specified number of professionally edited, high-resolution images. Image selection and editing are at the Photographer's discretion and reflect the Photographer's artistic style. RAW or unedited images are not provided.

3. Image Delivery & Turnaround: Final images will be delivered via a private online gallery within up to three (3) weeks from the session date. Rush delivery is available upon request for an additional fee and must be arranged in advance.

4. Additional Images & Prints: Additional edited images and professional, high-quality prints are available for purchase for all session types.

5. Portfolio & Promotional Use: The Photographer may use images from the photoshoot for portfolio, website, social media, and promotional purposes unless otherwise agreed in writing prior to the photoshoot.

6. Travel Fees: Travel within the Photographer's local service area (Bellevue to North Bend, WA) is included. Sessions outside this area may require an additional travel fee based on distance and will be disclosed prior to booking.

7. Rescheduling & Weather: Outdoor sessions are weather-dependent. In the event of unfavorable weather conditions, the session will be rescheduled at no additional charge. Clients are encouraged to notify the Photographer as early as possible if rescheduling is needed.

8. Usage Rights: Portrait, senior, elopement, and family sessions include personal usage rights only. Commercial or promotional use of images (including business branding, marketing, or events) requires additional licensing unless otherwise agreed upon in writing.

9. Sales Tax: Sales tax is not included in the listed service prices and will be calculated and added to the final invoice in accordance with applicable tax laws based on the Client's location.

10. Limitation of Liability: The Photographer is not responsible for circumstances beyond their control, including but not limited to weather conditions, venue restrictions, or client-related delays that may affect the session.

11. Agreement: By submitting payment or signing electronically, the Client acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agreed to this Photography Services Agreement in full.
